Our Vision

With a mission of providing medicines to the customer at an affordable rate, we at pharmafriend provide inquisitive price comparison of medicines requested by the customer either by means of a private e-medicine provider or by local medical shops and government medical Kendra's shops.

Other than this, pharmafriend would also provide alternative low-cost medication so that the user can choose them if viable and approved by the prescription Doctor, the same for which an email will be sent to the Doctor so that he/she can approve the alternative medicine.

How We Started

The idea of starting pharmafriend dates back when one of the pharmafriend’s cofounder I.e Devansh Agrahari was buying medicines from a private online medicine provider, while buying medicine he figured out that there were at least two issues anyone would face while buying from an online seller

I.e., first is pricing of the given medicine, it is different at different online medicine providing platforms, the other issue was of not providing an alternative medicine to given searched medicine when the given medicine is not available. so the next day Devansh discussed these issues with his other colleagues i.e., Akash and Karunesh, and after a much long discussion, the team came with a one stop solution to all these problems I.e., developing an application which would provide price comparison either by means of a private e-medicine provider or by local medical shops and government medical Kendra's shops and also providing a alternative low-cost medication so that the user can choose them if viable and approved by the prescription Doctor, the same for which an email will be sent to the Doctor so that he/she can approve the alternative medicine and this is how it all started!
